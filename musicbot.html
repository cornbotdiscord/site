CornMusicBot as a servers' primary Music bot

Part of every Discord server is keeping your members engaged with changes all of the time. One way to keep your Voice activity up is when you use a bot or activity to rise up hours.

Using a music bot can boost up hours drastically, when members get together and listen to music, or when members just hang out in VC listening to songs.

CornMusicBot is known to drastically increase voice channel activity when used regularly by members. Sounds great, customizable, and is just overall great.

If you need more channels with music, you need to host your own. You can do this by hosting it from the original owner's GitHub.
